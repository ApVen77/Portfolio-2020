About Me

My name is April Long. I have been an active teacher and teacher trainer for over 10 years. received a Bachelor's degree from University of Colorado where I studied Education with a focus on African American History, I later attended Udacity by Google for a Nano-degree in Frontend Development. I then attended The University of Denver to study fullstack development.
After focusing on web development, I knew that my place was still in the classroom. I have a passion for teaching which I've exuded for a decade. I can bring diversity, creativity, and consistency to any place I am employed! Technology is the future, so let's go in on it together!
